Algorithms

Simply put an algorithm is a list of directions which explain how to do a task. An algorithm is instructions for a computer on how to complete a task. Algorithms are not just a computer term but are also used in everyday life. A cake recipe could be considered an algorithm as it has step by step instructions on how to complete the task of making a cake. On computers, algorism can be used to create things like lists of people that belong on a no-fly list or create recommendations on Netflix.

Positive Impacts Algorithms Have

  1. Algorithms can have positive impacts on the world. They are highly efficiency because they can reduce work time for people. They do this by working much quicker than a person can which can speed up the process of a task. They can help with issues like reducing pollution and fixing transportation issues. The do this by identifying congestion and then providing a different quicker path to a location.
  2. Algorithms also have a positive impact on police, government and banks. It can improve police work by identifying high crime areas to send police to work. It has a positive impact on the government because an automated process can help with monitoring and regulation. This includes things like traffic offences and it can cut down on bureaucracy. It also helps banks because it allows more people to get loans because the more data the bank has the less risk the have meaning the can hand out more loans.
